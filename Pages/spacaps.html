COMPARE SPA CUPS

Benefits

✓ Voltage: 110V 2 Levels Temperature Control and Very Safe to Use.
✓ Solve Problems of Various Hair: Hair care SPA cap can effectively reduce hair damage, solve hair ends spilt and hair frizz, and deeply nourish your hair. Make your hair look smooth and silky.
✓ High-performance protection: It's a flame-retardant electronic temperature control heating cap, when the temperature reaches a certain level, the power will be cut off automatically, offer the maximum comfort and safety for you.
✓ Unique Design: Equipped with a high quality plastic film, easy to use and detach, extra waterproof and anti-electric. Auto-system to uncomment and recontact for security and best experience in use.
✓ Powerful Performance: It adopts silicone heating (charcoal fiber) and non-woven flame retardant treatment. It will make your hair evenly heated in oil or conditioner, and can also apply to hair care at home, promote absorption of nutrients and effectively shorten the dyeing time.


HOW TO USE

1. Wash your hair and dry with towel
2. Apply essential oil or conditioner to your hair
3. Put on shower cap then your heated spa cap, plug in with 20min power
4. Leave on for 20 min or 15 minutes
5. Turn off the power supply, take off the spa cap and wash your hair with warm water

Overview

Spa Cap provides uniform heat distribution and warms consistently to ensure deep penetration and absorption of the hair treatment into your scalp, thus ensuring that you get the best out of your hair treatment and conditioner.

Maintenance Tips

It's best to use warm water to wipe off any debris. Try to avoid harsh soaps as this may dry out the spa cap over time. To look after your cover long term we recommend using a specialised vinyl cleaner. You can use a vinyl protection spray on the outside of the cover, this will keep it looking brand new.
