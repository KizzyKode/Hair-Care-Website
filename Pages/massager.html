COMPARE MASSAGERS

GENTLE ON SCALP, TOUGH ON DIRT: made of elasticity silicone bristles which are soft enough to be gentle on your scalp without pulling your hair, they are also firm enough to provide a great massage and plenty of lather to really deeply cleanse your hair. It has just the right amount of firmness to not damage the scalp but enough to invigorate, and long enough easily reaches every inch of your scalp

Benefits

1. Removes dry skin that leads to dreaded dandruff
2. Strengthen the roots for healthy hair growth
3. Helps prevent the build-up of hair products
4. Removes greasy and flakey skin
5. Restore brilliance to the hair
6. Improves blood circulation
7. Help ease itchines -> DEEP CLEANSING: This helps to deeply cleanse your hair during the washing process and prevents the build-up of hair products or dry skin which leads to dandruff. It’s a decent tool to massage the shampoo right down to the scalp to which better than using fingers/nails which is too harsh and scratchy on the scalp. It lathers up so much more than finger tips and no more cracked fingernails


HOW TO USE

1. Wet your hair thoroughly with water.
2. Pour out a dime-sized amount of shampoo on your scalp massager brush.
3. Massage it into your scalp until it lathers.
4. Using gentle pressure try to massage your scalp in round motions.
5. Massage it well to calm sore patches of your scalp.

Overview

If you’ve ever had a scalp massage, you no doubt recall how relaxing it felt. Besides easing stress and tension, there’s also buzz around scalp massages being able to promote hair growth. Follow the tips below to maintain your massager and get the most from it

Maintenance Tips


✓ To maintain your rubber massager, remove any hair strands by hand, carefully so as not to break the rubber bristles.
✓ Gently cleanse the massager with soap and water.
✓ Rinse the rubber completely and let air dry with rubber bristles facing down.
✓ Never use petroleum or oil on rubber or it will ruin the rubber.
